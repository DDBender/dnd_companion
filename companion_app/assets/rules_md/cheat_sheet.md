## Combat Basics

Attack Roll: 1d20+Base Attack Bonus (BAB)+Ability Modifier+Size Modifier+Misc

Melee Modifier: Strength

Ranged Modifier: Dexterity (plus Range Penalty: -2 per range increment)

Armor Class (AC): 10+Armor Bonus+Shield+Dexterity+Size+Natural Armor(+other)

Touch AC: 10+Dexterity+Size(+other)

Flat-Footed AC: 10+Armor Bonus+Shield+Size+Natural Armor(+other-Dodge)

Critical Hit: If natural roll is in threat range (e.g., 19-20), roll again to confirm. If second roll hits AC, deal multiplied damage.

## The Combat Round

Each round, you can take one Standard + one Move action OR one Full-Round action.

| Action Type | Examples |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Standard** | Single attack, Cast a spell, Use a special ability. |
| **Move** | Move speed, Draw weapon, Open door, Load light crossbow. |
| **Full-Round** | Full attack (if BAB +6 or more), Charge, Run, Withdraw. |
| **Swift/Immediate** | Quickened spells, specific class feats (1 per round). |
| **Free** | Drop item, Speak, Drop prone. |

## Common Tactical Modifiers

Flanking: +2 bonus to Melee attacks if the enemy is between you and an ally.

High Ground: +1 bonus to Melee attacks.

Cover: +4 AC bonus.

Concealment (Blur/Fog): 20% miss chance.

Prone: -4 to Melee attacks; +4 AC vs Ranged / -4 AC vs Melee.

## Attacks of Opportunity (AoO)

Triggered by moving out of a threatened square or performing "distracting" acts (Casting, Ranged attacks, Unarmed attacks, Drinking a potion).

Limit: Usually 1 per round (unless you have Combat Reflexes).

Note: A 5-foot step (if no other movement is taken) does not trigger an AoO.

| Action | Provokes? | Notes |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Standard Attack** | No | Unless using Unarmed Strike without Improved Unarmed Strike. |
| **Ranged Attack** | Yes | Using a bow, crossbow, or throwing weapon in melee. |
| **Casting a Spell** | Yes | Unless "Casting Defensively" (Concentration check). |
| **Drinking a Potion** | Yes | Or oils, or feeding a potion to an unconscious ally. |
| **Moving** | Yes | Leaving a threatened square (5-foot step is safe). |
| **Pick up Item** | Yes | Picking something up from the ground. |
| **Stand up** | Yes | Moving from Prone to Standing. |
| **Combat Maneuver** | Yes | Grapple, Trip, Disarm, Sunder (unless you have the feat). |

## Grappling & Maneuvers

Grapple/Trip/Disarm: Most provoke an AoO unless you have the "Improved" feat.

Grapple Check: 1d20+BAB+Str Modifier+Special Size Modifier

Size Modifiers: Colossal +16, Gargantuan +12, Huge +8, Large +4, Medium +0, Small -4.

## Health & Dying

Disabled (0 HP): Can take one Move or Standard action; Standard action deals 1 damage to self after completion.

Dying (-1 to -9 HP): Lose 1 HP per round. 10% chance to "Stabilize" naturally.

Dead (-10 HP): Character is deceased.

## Spellcasting Mechanics

Casting Time: Usually 1 Standard Action.

### Casting Defensively 

To avoid an Attack of Opportunity (AoO), make a Concentration check. If you fail, the spell is lost.

Check: 1d20 + Concentration≥15+Spell Level

### Concentration Check (Taking Damage)

Required if hit while casting or during a "Damage over Time" effect.

Check: 1d20 + Concentration≥10+Damage Taken+Spell Level

### Spell Resistance (SR)

If the target has SR, the caster must "pierce" it.

Caster Level Check: 1d20+Caster Level≥Target’s SR

## Saving Throws

Used to resist spells, traps, or poisons.

Save Roll: 1d20+Base Save+Ability Modifier+Misc

| Save Type | Key Ability | Resists... |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Fortitude** | Constitution | Poison, Disease, Disintegration, Paralysis. |
| **Reflex** | Dexterity | Fireball, Lightning Bolt, Falling Traps. |
| **Will** | Wisdom | Charms, Illusions, Fear, Mind Control. |

## Difficulty Class (DC)

Spell Save DC: The number the enemy must roll to resist your spell.

Formula: 10+Spell Level+Caster’s Ability Modifier

Ability Modifiers: Intelligence (Wizard), Wisdom (Cleric/Druid), Charisma (Sorcerer/Bard).

## Magic Rules Summary

### Touch Attacks

Some spells require an attack roll.

Melee Touch: 1d20+BAB+Str (Targets ignore Armor, Shield, and Natural Armor).

Ranged Touch: 1d20+BAB+Dex (Targets ignore Armor, Shield, and Natural Armor).

### Counterspelling

Requires a Readied Action. You must cast the exact same spell (or Dispel Magic) to negate the enemy's spell.

### Component Types 

V (Verbal/Speech), S (Somatic/Gestures), M (Material), F (Focus).

Note: Wearing Armor applies a "Spell Failure %" to Arcane spells with Somatic (S) components.

## Common Status Conditions

| Condition | Description & Key Penalties |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Ability Damage** | Temporary loss of points; 1 pt/day recovery. 0 Strength = Helpless; 0 Dex = Paralyzed. |
| **Blinded** | -2 AC; lose Dex bonus to AC; move at half speed; -4 on Search and most Str/Dex checks. |
| **Confused** | Actions determined by 1d10 roll (1: Wander, 2-6: Do nothing, 7-9: Attack nearest, 10: Act normal). |
| **Cowering** | No actions; -2 AC; loses Dex bonus to AC. |
| **Dazed** | No actions; can still defend (no AC penalty). |
| **Dazzled** | -1 penalty on attack rolls, Search, and Spot checks. |
| **Dead** | Soul leaves body; cannot be reached by normal healing. |
| **Deafened** | -4 Initiative; 20% spell failure for verbal components; fails Listen checks. |
| **Disabled** | 0 HP (or stable at negative HP). Can take only 1 Move or Standard action; strenuous actions deal 1 dmg. |
| **Dying** | Negative HP (-1 to -9). Unconscious; loses 1 HP/round unless stabilized (10% chance). |
| **Energy Drained** | Gains negative levels. -1 to attacks, saves, skills, and -5 HP per level. |
| **Entangled** | -2 on attacks; -4 to Dex; move at half speed; DC 15 + spell level Concentration check to cast. |
| **Exhausted** | -6 to Str and Dex; move at half speed. 1 hour rest reduces to Fatigued. |
| **Fatigued** | -2 to Str and Dex; cannot run or charge. 8 hours rest removes. |
| **Flat-Footed** | Has not acted yet in combat. Loses Dex bonus to AC and cannot make AoOs. |
| **Frightened** | Must flee from source; -2 on attacks, saves, and checks. |
| **Grappled** | Cannot move or cast (without check); loses Dex bonus to AC vs. those not grappling. |
| **Helpless** | Bound, sleeping, paralyzed, or unconscious. Dex is 0 (-5 AC); Melee attacks get +4 bonus. |
| **Incorporeal** | No physical body; immune to nonmagical attacks; 50% miss chance from magic weapons. |
| **Invisible** | +2 on attacks vs. sighted; ignore target's Dex bonus to AC. |
| **Nauseated** | Only a single Move action per turn; cannot attack or cast. |
| **Panicked** | Drops gear and flees at top speed; -2 on all saves/checks; Cowering if cornered. |
| **Paralyzed** | Str/Dex effectively 0 (Helpless); cannot move or act; purely mental actions okay. |
| **Petrified** | Turned to stone (Helpless). Unaware of surroundings; weight increases 10x. |
| **Pinned** | Held firmly in a grapple. Cannot move; -4 AC vs. outsiders; loses Dex bonus to AC. |
| **Prone** | -4 on melee attacks; +4 AC vs. ranged; -4 AC vs. melee. Standing is a Move action. |
| **Shaken** | -2 penalty on attack rolls, saving throws, and skill checks. |
| **Sickened** | -2 penalty on all attack rolls, weapon damage rolls, saves, and checks. |
| **Stunned** | Drops items; no actions; -2 AC; loses Dex bonus to AC. |
| **Unconscious** | Helpless and knocked out (0 HP or nonlethal damage > current HP). |

## Quick Reference: Fear Progression

Fear effects in 3.5e usually stack:

Shaken: -2 penalties.

Frightened: -2 penalties + Must flee.

Panicked: -2 penalties + Must flee + Drop all held items.

## Condition "Special" States

Invisible: +2 bonus on attack rolls vs sighted targets; targets lose Dex bonus to AC.

Staggered: Only one Standard or Move action per round (occurs when nonlethal damage equals current HP).
